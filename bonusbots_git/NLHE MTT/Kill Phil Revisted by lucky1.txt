// Post Flop Play  
// If you flop 2-pair, 3-of-a-kind, a set, a straight, a flush, a full house, 4-of-a-kind, or a straight flush, move all-in when it’s your turn to act; otherwise check, and fold if there’s a bet. The one caveat is that the 2-pair must use two cards from your hand that match two cards on the board. A hand such as 9-7 with a flop of 9-7-2 qualifies; a hand such as T-T with a flop of 9-9-2 doesn’t. If everyone checks on the flop, you get a free look at the turn card.


// If you flop top pair and best kicker, 2 Pair, 3 of a Kind, Set, Straight, or flush bet or raise allin
when bets <= 1
when ((HaveTopPair and HaveBestKicker) or (HaveOverPair and not paironboard) or (HaveTwoPair and not pairinhand) or HaveTrips or HaveSet or HaveStraight or havefullhouse or HaveFlush or HaveQuads or HaveStraightFlush) raisemax force  

// If there's a bet and a raise before it gets to you…
// * Move in with a set or better.
// * Move in with top 2-pair if the flop doesn't contain three consecutive cards or three cards of the same suit.
// * Move in with 3-of-a-kind if you have top kicker in your hand.
when bets = 1 and raises >= 1 and botslastaction = none
when (HaveSet or HaveStraight or HaveFlush or havefullhouse or HaveQuads or HaveStraightFlush) raisemax force  // move in with set or better
when HaveTopTwoPair and not (FlushPossible or StraightPossible) raisemax force  // move in with top 2-pair if the flop doesn't contain three consecutive cards or three cards of the same suit.
when HaveTrips and HaveBestKicker raisemax force  // move in withh 3 of a kind (trips) when you have best kicker

// If you flop an open-ended straight or a flush draw using both your hole cards and there's been no action when it gets to you…
// *Move in with two overcards to the board.
// *Move in with the nut flush draw.
// *Move in with a pair in addition to your flush draw.
// *Otherwise, check your draw, fold if your opponent bets.
// *If he checks, move in on the turn if you make the straight or flush.
when (HaveStraightDraw and (not haveinsidestraightdraw)) and bets = 0 and raises = 0 and botslastaction = none
when overcards = 2 raisemax force  // move in with 2 overcards to the board
when HavenutFlushDraw raisemax force  // move in with nutFlushDraw					  
when others  and bets = 1 fold force  // otherwise, check your draw, fold if your opponent bets.
