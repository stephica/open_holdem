// if we RaiseMax its possible that all 3 holding a good pocket or an AK/AQ will pick us holding KK 
// or AA due to our overbet and find a fold. I strongly doubt that 
// all of them either fold or call us here (much more likely that at least one will shove) and even 
// then they only have a 1/8 shot of making their set. We then shove all in on flop unless pair of J Q or K come up.
